Nerds Don't Understand Popular Lingo

05-06-2026
By Sofia Maldonado

This morning I was reading this article on The Verge about Microsoft's new AI models and strategy, and while reading I noticed this line:

For years, Microsoft’s AI business leaned hard on its early and exclusive partnership with OpenAI. But the drama-filled marriage slowly devolved into a situationship, and the pair effectively separated in late April (though Microsoft is still OpenAI’s primary cloud partner — for now).

I really find it funny when nerds misuse words that are popular among "normie" populations, like situationship, but in their defense most people that do use the word don't know what if means either. I would rather drink motor oil than to describe my relationship with someone as a situationship.
