My personal Music Hall of Fame (Salón de la Fama [SDLF] in spanish) is a collection of my favorite songs that I started in January 2025. You can think of this as my personal canon of the songs that I love most.

The premise is simple: [1] I only add 16 songs at the end of every month, creating a monthly "class" like in sports hall of fames. [2] I can only add two songs by a specific artist per class, and only 1 song per album to a specific class, to promote variety. [3] Songs only become "eligible" for the hall until the month after I first heard them, to avoid recency bias.

The first 3 classes, the classes of 2022, 2023 and 2024, are made up of my favorite songs that I listened to in those years. However, starting in January 2025, the classes are monthly, not yearly.
